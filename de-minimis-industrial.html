What We Do

Sourcing services that cover
the full procurement cycle

01

Component Sourcing

We identify and qualify suppliers for specific components — from standard catalog parts to custom specs. We handle the outreach, vetting, and sample coordination so your team doesn't have to manage it directly.

02

Supplier Matching

We maintain active relationships with component manufacturers across Taiwan and Asia — particularly those underrepresented in U.S. distribution. When you have a hard-to-source need, we have a shorter path.

How We Work

A clear process from requirement to delivery

01

Submit Your Requirement

Send us a part number, spec sheet, or description of what you need. Include quantity, timeline, and any technical constraints. No RFQ template required — we work with what you have.

02

Supplier Outreach & Matching

We review your requirement against our supplier network and initiate outreach to qualified manufacturers. We evaluate fit on spec, lead time, and supplier reliability before presenting options.

03

Structured Quote & Comparison

You receive a clear comparison of sourced options — pricing, lead time, certifications, and relevant spec notes. We flag concerns and recommend based on your application, not just price.

04

Order & Delivery Coordination

Once you approve, we handle supplier coordination, export documentation, and logistics. We keep you informed at each stage — no chasing required on your end.

05

Ongoing Support

For repeat components or ongoing programs, we maintain supplier relationships and coordinate subsequent orders. We track lead times and flag supply chain changes proactively.
