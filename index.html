What we do

More than getting
in the door

Most market entry services stop at compliance and introductions. We stay until your sales are actually growing — building the relationships, channels, and infrastructure for long-term US success.

01
Market Entry

We map the US landscape for your product — the right channels, buyers, price points, and regions to target first. Then we execute a structured entry plan built around growth, not just access.

02
Sales & Distribution

We source and develop the relationships that drive revenue — distributors, wholesalers, retailers, and direct accounts. We manage those relationships on your behalf so sales actually happen.

How we work

Hands-on,
start to

We work as an embedded US partner — not a factory of onboarded accounts, but a focused relationship where we're genuinely invested in your results.
